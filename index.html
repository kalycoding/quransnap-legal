Summary: QuranSnap processes audio locally on your device to identify Quran recitations. We do not store your audio recordings on our servers. Your privacy is our priority.

1. Introduction

Welcome to QuranSnap ("we," "our," or "us"). QuranSnap is a mobile application that identifies Quran recitations from audio clips and provides translations and commentary. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Audio Data

When you use QuranSnap's identification feature:

  • We access your device's microphone to capture audio of Quran recitations
  • Audio is processed to convert speech to text for identification purposes
  • Audio recordings are processed locally on your device and are not stored on our servers
  • Only the transcribed text is used to match against the Quran database

2.2 Subscription & Purchase Data

When you subscribe to QuranSnap Premium:

  • Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store)
  • We use RevenueCat to manage subscription status
  • We do not have access to your payment details (credit card numbers, etc.)
  • We receive only subscription status information (active/expired, plan type)

2.3 Usage Data

We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the app:

  • App crashes and error reports
  • Feature usage patterns (anonymized)
  • Device type and operating system version

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Identify Quran verses from audio recordings
  • Provide translations and tafsir (commentary)
  • Manage your subscription status
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Develop new features based on usage patterns

4. Data Storage & Security

Key Point: Your audio recordings are NOT uploaded to any server. All audio processing happens on your device.

We implement appropriate security measures to protect any data we do collect. Subscription data is encrypted and stored securely through our service providers.

5. Third-Party Services

QuranSnap uses the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat: Subscription management - Privacy Policy
  • Apple App Store / Google Play Store: Payment processing

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request information about data we hold about you
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out: Disable analytics through your device settings
  • Revoke Permissions: Disable microphone access at any time through your device settings

7. Children's Privacy

QuranSnap is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app's content is educational and religious in nature, appropriate for family use.

8. Microphone Permission

QuranSnap requires microphone access solely for the purpose of identifying Quran recitations. You can revoke this permission at any time through your device settings, though this will disable the core identification feature.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our practices, please contact us at:

Email: kalycodes@gmail.com
